Jumeirah Vittaveli — Complete Laundry Installation

Delivering a world-class laundry facility for one of the Maldives' most iconic luxury properties, marking the beginning of a long-standing partnership with EON Resorts.

Project Details

Client EON Resorts (Jumeirah Vittaveli)
Location South Male' Atoll, Maldives
Scope Complete Laundry Installation
Brands Used FAGOR, GHIDINI

The Challenge

Jumeirah Vittaveli is one of the Maldives' most prestigious luxury resorts, demanding nothing short of excellence in every operational aspect. When EON Resorts needed a partner to supply and install a complete laundry facility, the equipment had to meet the exacting standards expected by the Jumeirah brand — reliability, capacity and efficiency were non-negotiable.

Operating in the Maldives brings unique logistical challenges. Every piece of equipment must be transported by sea to a remote island location, and installation teams must work within tight commissioning windows to minimise disruption to resort operations.

Our Solution

GTS delivered a complete turnkey laundry solution, encompassing:

  • Full supply of industrial washing machines, dryers and ironing equipment from FAGOR (Spain)
  • Complementary cleaning and finishing accessories from GHIDINI (Italy)
  • Professional installation by GTS's in-house technical team
  • Full commissioning and performance testing
  • On-site training for the resort's laundry staff

The Result

The laundry facility was delivered on time and to specification. The combination of FAGOR's proven industrial laundry systems and GHIDINI's precision cleaning equipment gave Jumeirah Vittaveli a laundry operation capable of handling the demands of a five-star resort.

This project marked the beginning of a partnership with EON Resorts that would go on to span five consecutive laundry installations across their portfolio — a testament to the quality and reliability GTS delivers.

Long-Term Impact

The success of the Jumeirah Vittaveli installation established GTS as EON Resorts' trusted equipment partner. The relationship that began here led directly to subsequent projects at Maadhoo Island, OBLU Helengeli and beyond — each one awarded on the strength of the work that came before it.
