OBLU Helengeli — Laundry Installation

The fifth consecutive laundry installation for EON Resorts — by this point, GTS wasn't just a supplier, we were part of the team.

Project Details

Client EON Resorts (OBLU by Atmosphere)
Location Helengeli, North Male' Atoll
Scope Complete Laundry Installation
Project Number 5th consecutive for EON Resorts

A Partnership at Full Stride

By the time EON Resorts engaged GTS for the OBLU Helengeli project, the working relationship had matured into something deeper than a standard vendor arrangement. Five projects in, both teams understood each other's standards, processes and expectations. This familiarity translated directly into efficiency — shorter lead times, smoother installations and fewer coordination issues.

The OBLU Brand Standard

OBLU by Atmosphere operates as a contemporary, all-inclusive resort brand. While the positioning differs from the ultra-luxury segment, the operational demands on a laundry facility remain substantial. High guest turnover and an all-inclusive model mean consistently high laundry volumes — the equipment must be industrial-grade and built to last.

What We Delivered

  • Industrial laundry equipment sized for the resort's all-inclusive guest capacity
  • Equipment specification refined from lessons learned across four prior EON Resorts installations
  • Seamless logistics and island delivery coordination
  • Installation, commissioning and team training — executed by technicians who had done this four times before for the same client

The Power of Consistency

What makes five consecutive projects remarkable is not the number itself — it's what the number represents. Each time EON Resorts expanded their portfolio, they had the option to go to market and evaluate alternatives. Five times, they chose GTS. That decision was based on direct experience: equipment that performs, installations that stay on schedule and a partner that stands behind their work.

In the Maldives, your reputation is your most valuable asset. Every project either builds it or erodes it. Five consecutive projects with the same client is the clearest evidence that GTS delivers on its promises.
