Project Case Study 2014

Dhoores Retreat — Laundry Installation & Linen Supply

A comprehensive laundry and linen package combining Spanish engineering with premium Irish textiles for Vermillion Resorts.

Project Details

Client Vermillion Resorts (Dhoores Retreat)
Location Maldives
Scope Laundry Installation & Linen Supply
Brands Used FAGOR, Lissadel Liddell (Ireland)

The Challenge

Vermillion Resorts required a turnkey solution for their Dhoores Retreat property — not just laundry equipment, but a complete package that included premium-quality linen. The property needed to launch operations with everything in place, from industrial washing machines to bed sheets that matched the quality guests expect from a Maldivian resort.

Our Solution

GTS provided a comprehensive, end-to-end package:

  • Full supply and installation of FAGOR industrial laundry equipment, including washers, dryers and finishing equipment
  • Premium linen supply from Lissadel Liddell of Ireland — one of the most respected names in hospitality textiles
  • Complete commissioning with performance validation
  • Staff training on equipment operation and linen care best practices

Why This Project Mattered

The Dhoores Retreat project demonstrated GTS's ability to go beyond equipment supply. By pairing industrial laundry machinery with a curated linen package, GTS delivered a single-source solution that simplified procurement, reduced coordination overhead and ensured compatibility between the equipment and the textiles it would process.

This integrated approach — equipment plus consumables — became a model for future GTS projects, where clients increasingly valued the convenience and reliability of working with a single trusted partner.

The Outcome

Dhoores Retreat launched with a fully operational laundry facility and a complete linen inventory, ready to serve guests from day one. The FAGOR equipment continues to deliver reliable performance, while the Lissadel Liddell linen upholds the quality standards that guests associate with the Maldives hospitality experience.
