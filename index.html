About GTS

Two Decades of Hospitality Excellence

GTS Private Limited has been the equipment partner of choice for the Maldives hospitality industry since 2004.

From supplying single spare parts to designing and commissioning complete commercial kitchens and laundry facilities, GTS serves the full spectrum of resort and hotel needs — from boutique guesthouses to the world's most prestigious hotel chains.

With a team carrying over 17 years of combined hospitality industry experience, and a dedicated technical team with over 20 years of specialised expertise, GTS is more than a supplier — we are a long-term operational partner for every property we serve.

Our clients trust us because we don't just deliver equipment. We ensure it runs.

Our Vision

To build a reputation as the most reliable supplier of hotel equipment and machinery, serving the tourism industry of the Maldives through dedication, consistency and quality.

Our Mission

To build trust among our clients for our products and services, and to position GTS as the leading supplier of equipment and machinery required for the hospitality industry of the Maldives.
